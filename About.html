Our Story

The Millenial Alley store project started in 2018 with a dream to bridge this gap in the streetwear scene. There wasn’t really a proper store around that “speaks” to the demographic; everything before this was via online platforms and ‘fly-by-night’ stores. Well Bred hopes to be able to introduce, share and grow the scene here and around the region. Our stores have, in this short span of time garnered a strong cult following from Hypebeast KOL (Key Opinion Leader) types to discerning taste-makers. We aim to bring a vibrant 360-shopping experience for the hypebeast and trendies. New-wave brands like Acme De La Vie (ADLV), FOG Essentials, #FR2 Fxxking Rabbits, Pleasures, Chinatown Market CTM, Poshbrain etc line up side by side with Classics like Champion, RipNDip, Carhartt WIP and the likes. Whether it is our physical shops or online store, we hope to play the game right. Millenial Alley hopes to be that right store that keeps every demographic happy. We may work WITHIN this culture, but end of the day, we also want to work FOR this culture. Peace out.
